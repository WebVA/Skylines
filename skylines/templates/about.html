Welcome to SkyLines, the internet platform for sharing flights!

The major goals of the Skylines-Project are:

  • To provide a free platform for sharing and analysing flights
  • Skylines is based on free software (License).
  • The stored flights are available for everybody without any limitations.
  • Skylines is a non-commercial project, there are no ads on the webpage, its free of charge.
  • Skylines has Live Tracking, let your friends parcitipate on your adventures.
  • Skylines has also some social network aspects with comments, notifications, friends and followers.
  • Airport and Waypoint data is provided by the WELT2000 project (License)

Current status: under development
